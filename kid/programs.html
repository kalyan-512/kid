Our Programs

We Meet Kids at Their Level Regardless of Their Age

(1.5-3 years)

Toddler

A gentle introduction to the world outside home. Music, stories, and sensory play build comfort and social skills in a safe, nurturing environment designed specifically for our youngest learners.

(3-4years)

Nursery

Through free play, art, and group activities, children develop independence, confidence, and friendships. Our curriculum emphasizes creativity, exploration, and early social development.

(4-5 years)

Junior KG

Introduces a formal learning environment with early literacy, numeracy, and social skills development. Children begin to develop strong foundations in reading, writing, and mathematical concepts.

(5-6 years)

Senior KG

Prepares your child for formal schooling with advanced reading, writing, and problem-solving skills. Our comprehensive curriculum ensures children are confident and ready for primary education.
