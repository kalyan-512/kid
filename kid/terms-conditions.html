4. Attendance and Schedule

Operating Hours

Our preschool operates Monday to Saturday from 8:00 AM to 7:00 PM. We are closed on Sundays and public holidays. Special closures will be communicated in advance.

Drop-off and Pick-up

  • Children must be dropped off and picked up by authorized persons only
  • Parents must notify us in advance if someone else will pick up their child
  • Late pick-up may result in additional charges
  • Repeated late pick-ups may result in termination of enrollment

Absences

Please notify us if your child will be absent. No tuition adjustments will be made for absences, vacations, or illness.

5. Health and Safety

Illness Policy

  • Children who are ill should not be brought to school
  • Children with fever, vomiting, diarrhea, or contagious conditions must stay home
  • Children must be symptom-free for 24 hours before returning
  • We reserve the right to send home any child who appears unwell

Medication

Staff may administer medication only with written parental consent and clear instructions. All medications must be in original containers with proper labeling.

Emergency Procedures

In case of medical emergency, we will attempt to contact parents immediately. If unreachable, we will contact emergency contacts and seek appropriate medical care. Parents are responsible for all medical expenses incurred.

Allergies and Dietary Restrictions

Parents must inform us of any allergies, dietary restrictions, or special needs. We will make reasonable accommodations but cannot guarantee a completely allergen-free environment.
